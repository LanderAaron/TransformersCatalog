Welcome to my Transformers Catalog website! Here, you'll find already added characters of different Autobots and Decepticons. The point of this is to understand arrays and objects
for my understanding and manipulating the data based on the user's intent. 

The second point of this program is to build a database of Transformer bots because as you can see, Transformers is one of my most favorite interests.
This website is supposed to imitate what an Iacon Bot Database website would look like in the Transformers universe. Iacon is the fictional Transformers City Capitol and is 
known to store the Hall of Records or library, a place where a Cybertronian can indulge in vast knowledge in about almost every thing, except in web form!

It was overall a very fun, but tedious project. Spent about 3-4 hours a day for about 4 days designing, structuring, testing, and mostly debugging the website.

I will continue to expand and add features, but the main outgoing features are as follows. 

The user can:
1. Sort bots alphabetically A-Z.
2. Sort bots by prioritizing Autobots or Decepticons first.
3. Choose the default sorting.
4. Filter out to see only Autobots or Decepticons and choose to see all bots.
5. Search for specific bots' names using the search bar and initiating the search using either the search icon or hitting enter.

Known issues:
1. The navigation bar is for looks and unfortunately doesn't function... yet.
2. The accessibility and privacy policy links are also a little bit non-functional, but clickable. They will redirect you to one entertaining and one funny video ;)
3. Images are a little bit inconsistent with their sizing as they were grabbed from various sources.
